Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

NemJPG does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All image processing happens entirely on your device.

Data Collection

NemJPG collects absolutely no data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered
  • No crash reports are sent
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No third-party services or SDKs are integrated
  • No network connections are made by the app
  • No account or sign-in is required

Image Processing

All image conversion is performed entirely on your device using native system frameworks. On macOS, NemJPG uses Apple's Core Image and Core Graphics. On Windows, NemJPG uses the built-in Windows Imaging Component (WIC).

Your images are read from disk, converted locally, and saved to your chosen location. At no point are images or any derived data uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

Third-Party Sharing

NemJPG does not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share.

Children's Privacy

NemJPG does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Given that NemJPG collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
