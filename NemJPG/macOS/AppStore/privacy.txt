NemJPG Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

== DATA COLLECTION ==

NemJPG does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.

== DETAILED PRIVACY INFORMATION ==

- No personal information is collected
- No usage data or analytics are collected
- No crash reports are sent
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used
- No third-party services or SDKs are integrated
- No network connections are made by the app
- No account or sign-in is required
- No data is stored outside of the app's local sandbox

== IMAGE PROCESSING ==

All image processing is performed entirely on-device using Apple's native frameworks (Core Image, Core Graphics). Images are read from disk, converted locally, and saved to the user's chosen location. At no point are images or any derived data uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

== THIRD-PARTY SHARING ==

NemJPG does not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share.

== CHILDREN'S PRIVACY ==

NemJPG does not collect any data from any user, including children.
