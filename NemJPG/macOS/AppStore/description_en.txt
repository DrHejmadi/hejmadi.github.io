Batch Image Converter

== FULL DESCRIPTION ==
NemJPG is a fast, free batch image converter for macOS. Drag and drop your images or entire folders, choose your output format and quality, and convert hundreds of images in seconds.

FEATURES:
- Convert 20+ image formats to JPG, PNG, WebP, or TIFF
- Drag and drop files and folders
- Batch convert hundreds of images at once
- Adjustable quality: High (95%), Medium (80%), Web (70%), Compressed (50%)
- Optional image resizing with preserved aspect ratio
- Automatic white background for transparent images
- File size report showing space saved
- Clean, native macOS interface built with SwiftUI
- Dark mode support

SUPPORTED INPUT FORMATS:
PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, WebP, ICO, HEIC, HEIF, AVIF, PSD, JP2, and RAW camera formats (CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG, ORF, RAW).

PRIVACY:
NemJPG processes all images locally on your Mac. No data is collected, uploaded, or shared. No account required. No internet connection needed.

NemJPG is free and open source.
