About

General practitioner focused on the future of healthcare

I am a specialist in general medicine and co-owner of Lægehuset Ferritslev on the island of Funen, Denmark. I also serve on the board of People's Doctor. My daily work revolves around seeing patients and ensuring quality care – but also exploring how technology can improve healthcare for both patients and staff.

Together with Andreas Pihl I have written the "Doctor Hansen" series, which translates complex health technology into a language everyone can understand – from policymakers to patients.
